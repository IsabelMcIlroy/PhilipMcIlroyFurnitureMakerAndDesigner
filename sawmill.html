Tree to Furniture

Do you have a beautiful hardwood tree in your yard that has to be removed? These trees can often make incredible pieces of furniture that will preserve the memory of your tree forever. I have a sawmill that can process your tree into boards that will then be dried before being made into a unique piece of furniture.
If you are interested in this service, please send a picture of your tree or log and I can advise you on what I might be able to create out of it!
