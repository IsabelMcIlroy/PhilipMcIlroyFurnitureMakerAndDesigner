Item Details

Pricing varies slightly depending on the wood species required. Most pieces are made to order just for you, so lead times depend on how busy I am. Please contact me and I can give you a quote and completion date.

Materials

My furniture is offered in a range of North American hardwoods. As well I do offer some local Newfoundland hardwoods from my own sawmill depending on availability. If you wish to order a piece of furniture, we can discuss what material will work best for you and provide samples if required.

Finishes

My furniture is finished with a hard wax oil that provides a durable nontoxic finish that can be easily repaired if a piece is scuffed or damaged. A light sanding of a damaged area and a spot of fresh oil and it will look as good as new.

Furniture can be cleaned with a damp cloth or mild soap solution as necessary. Avoid strong cleaning products and abrasives.

If you ever have any problems with the craftsmanship of your furniture please let me know and I will repair or replace as appropriate. If you damage the finish or the piece has obviously been misused, I can still repair it for you however I may have to charge for the service.

As each piece is made just for you, the best way to order is by emailing or calling me. Then we can work together to determine materials, customizations, and timelines.
